Intro

Hi, I'm Vincent. If you're interested in recording your music or having a song created, then you're in the right place. I have experience writing and recording songs as a solo artist and with my band 'Tenmoku' as the lead singer and drummer. Here in my home studio I enjoy producing music in collaboration with others and encouraging other artists with their own music projects. View feedback from previuos clients below

Services

Song Writing

Song writing is a great passion of mine and I enjoy the creative process. My services include:

  • - Have a song written for a special occasion in the sytle of your choosing.
  • - Help creating a song or developing your music ideas, e.g. with melodies or lyrics.

Recording

The studio is an open space to anyone interested in recording. My services include:

  • - Record yourself onto a song I write for you.
  • - Record your own music.
  • - Practice recording in a studio setting.
  • - Teaching children or adults about sound recording using apple software (Garageband or Logix Pro).

Studio

In the studio I use Apple’s Logic Pro X as the recording software. I also use the Focusrite Scarlett 18i20 audio interface together with both condenser and dynamic mics. I have a Roland KV11 electronic drum kit, midi keyboard, guitars & bass, also headphones and a PA system for playback listening.
